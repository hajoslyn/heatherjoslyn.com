About Heather Joslyn

I'm an editor and writer at The Chronicle of Philanthropy, covering the charitable nonprofit world. Previously, I worked at City Paper, Baltimore's alt-weekly, and Whittle Communications, a former multimedia company in Knoxville, Tenn. I hold a bachelor's degree from the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University. I geek out over black-and-white movies, guitars, standup comedy, American history, rock 'n' roll, and cooking from scratch. I live in Baltimore, the Greatest City in America.
